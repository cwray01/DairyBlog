SOP of Everything

2024-06-29

There is always a word “SOP” rush into my eyes, until one day I can’t not bare that I don’t know this word at all. So I looked up from wiki:

A standard operating procedure(SOP) is a set of step-by-step instrutions compiled by an organization to help workers carry out routine operations.

Obviously that is any boss want even in their dreams.
Because it is ‘standard’, Tom can do it, Jerry can do it, Laura can do it. On the one hand SOP reduces the cost of education the newbee, but ont the other hand it kills innovation of doing a work.

Honestly I don’t like SOP but unlucky there is little space within a huge system.

In 2021 SIFF me and Eloise watched a movie names ‘困在系统里的人’, the movie was so so but the name of the movie usually on my mind. When I complained to a system, I was literaly complained to bunch of code, and that is the current & future I can see.s No ways to flee.
